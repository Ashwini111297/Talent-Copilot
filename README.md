# TalentCopilot – AI-Powered Recruiting Assistant

## Overview

TalentCopilot is an AI-powered recruiting platform designed to help Talent Acquisition teams automate repetitive recruiting tasks such as candidate sourcing, resume screening, and interview scheduling.

The platform acts as a virtual recruiting assistant that helps recruiters identify suitable candidates, analyze resumes against job descriptions, and generate actionable hiring recommendations. Instead of manually reviewing hundreds of resumes, recruiters can upload a Job Description (JD) and candidate resumes, and the system will automatically extract relevant information, evaluate candidate fit, highlight strengths and gaps, and rank candidates based on job requirements.

The goal is to significantly reduce the time recruiters spend on sourcing and screening while improving hiring efficiency and candidate quality.

---

## Problem Statement

Recruiters spend a significant amount of time sourcing candidates, manually reviewing resumes, and evaluating candidate suitability against job requirements. This process is repetitive, time-consuming, and prone to inconsistencies.

TalentCopilot addresses these challenges by leveraging AI to automate sourcing, resume parsing, candidate evaluation, and hiring recommendations, enabling recruiters to focus on relationship building and strategic hiring decisions.

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Resume Analysis

- Upload resumes in PDF or DOCX format
- Extract candidate details such as:
  - Name
  - Experience
  - Skills
  - Education
  - Current Company
  - Notice Period
- Generate structured candidate profiles

### JD Matching Engine

- Upload or create a Job Description
- Compare candidate profiles against JD requirements
- Generate:
  - Match Score
  - Skill Match Analysis
  - Missing Skills
  - Experience Alignment
  - Hiring Recommendation

### AI Recruiter Notes

- Automatically create recruiter-friendly summaries
- Highlight candidate strengths and concerns
- Provide interview recommendations

### Candidate Ranking

- Analyze multiple resumes simultaneously
- Rank candidates based on relevance to the JD
- Display candidates in descending order of match score

### AI Sourcing Assistant (Future Enhancement)

- Search public professional profiles and candidate databases
- Recommend potential candidates based on JD requirements
- Act as an AI sourcing intern for recruiters

### Interview Scheduling (Future Enhancement)

- Integrate with Google Calendar and Outlook
- Suggest interview slots based on interviewer availability
- Automatically send interview invitations
